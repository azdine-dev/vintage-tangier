The Origin Story

Inspired by Tangier's legendary "Interzone" era, where artists, writers, and spies mingled over strong coffee, our kitchen brings together an international legacy with local authenticity.

We honor the past while crafting the future of coffee culture in Morocco. Every bean is selected with care, every cup brewed to perfection, and every corner of our space designed to transport you to a time when conversation was an art form.

A Journey Through Time

From the first spark of inspiration to our grand opening.

1920

The Inspiration

The seed is planted during the height of the International Zone era in Tangier. The cosmopolitan spirit of the city inspires a vision of a gathering place for all cultures.

1955

The Golden Era

Tangier reaches its cultural peak. Our family acquires the historic building in the Kasbah, preserving its architectural integrity for future generations.

2010

The Concept

Drafting the vision. We begin sourcing vintage equipment from Italy and establishing direct trade relationships with local coffee farmers.

2023

Grand Opening

Doors open. "Vintage Coffee & Kitchen" welcomes its first guests, blending historic ambiance with modern culinary techniques.
