Our Origins

Born from the Cultural Tapestry of Tangier

Vintage Coffee & Kitchen began as a whisper in the bustling alleyways of the Medina. We wanted to create a sanctuary that honors the rich history of Moroccan coffee culture while embracing the precision of the third-wave coffee movement.

Every tile in our kitchen and every cup on our shelf tells a story of craftsmanship and heritage. We aren't just serving coffee; we are curating an experience that transports you to a simpler, more elegant time.

The Kitchen

A Culinary Fusion

Our menu is a love letter to Tangier’s international spirit. We blend local, seasonal ingredients with recipes collected from around the world.

From our signature spiced shakshouka to our delicate French pastries, every dish is prepared with the same attention to detail as our espressos. We believe food should comfort the soul as much as it nourishes the body.

The Coffee Philosophy

We believe that every cup tells a story. From the altitude of the farm to the precision of the pour, our process is a dedication to quality.
