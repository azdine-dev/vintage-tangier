Our Story

Where Coffee Meets
Moroccan Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Tangier, Vintage Coffee & Kitchen brings together the rich traditions of Moroccan hospitality with the art of specialty coffee.

Our carefully curated menu features artisanal coffee blends, fresh pastries, and exquisite cuisine crafted with locally-sourced ingredients. Every cup tells a story, and every plate is a celebration of flavor.
