About Us

Our Story

Born from a love of vintage aesthetics and the vibrant culture of Tangier, Vintage Coffee & Kitchen is more than just a cafe. It's a bridge between the old-world charm of Morocco and the new wave of specialty coffee.

We source our beans ethically, bake our bread daily, and serve every cup with the warmth of traditional Moroccan hospitality.
